About Langmeier-Backup

Langmeier Software provides data security for companies with its backup software solutions . The focus is on backing up data, servers and computers – physical as well as virtual – and on the seamless recovery of these.

With its software solution "aBusiness", Langmeier Software makes the working lives of many people more productive..

Langmeier Software GmbH is based in Pfäffikon SZ and was founded in April 2004 by Urs Langmeier as a sole proprietorship, becoming a limited liability company in October 2008. In 2019, Langmeier Software celebrated its fifteenth anniversary.
